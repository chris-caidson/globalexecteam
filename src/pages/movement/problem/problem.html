Food Matters

Food Matters sheds a spotlight on the trillion dollar worldwide sickness industry and how OVER-FARMING and BIG PHARMA has had a detrimental impact on our health, putting the interest of PRIVATE PROFIT over PUBLIC HEALTH.

The Unhealthy Truth

The Unhealthy Truth is a call to action that shows how each of us can do our part and keep our own families safe. Author Robyn O’Brien turns to accredited research conducted in Europe that confirms the toxicity of America’s food supply, and investigates the relationship between Big Food and Big Money that has ensured that the United States is one of the only developed countries in the world to allow hidden toxins in our food—toxins that are increasingly being blamed for the alarming recent increases in allergies, A.D.H.D., cancer, and asthma among our children.

Why Diets Are Failing Us

Peter Greenlaw is the author of the best-selling book, Why Diets are failing us. He discusses the challenges of Toxicity, Deficiency, and GMO’s and their implications on our health. He also highlights the alarming concern of how there are over 100,000 chemicals in commercial use in North America today and less than 560 have ever been tested for human exposure or consumption.
