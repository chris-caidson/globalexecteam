IsaFYI: Jessica Johnston

Jessica Johnston

Sharing Health and Financial Hope Opens New Doors

Congratulations, Isagenix Millionaires No. 157, Jessica and Joe Johnston!

“I began my career in investment banking with a huge broker corporation and did really well there,” reflects 39-year-old Jessica. “But the job kept me overworked and stressed with the long hours.”

After facing some health challenges, the newly wed decided to quit her job and focus solely on her health, so she and her husband could start their family.

Splashing Around with Isagenix

One day while at the community pool with her daughters, Jessica found herself talking with her neighbor, Herb.

“Herb said, ‘I’d love for you to take a look at Isagenix,’” says Jessica. “He said, ‘Your personality would be great for the business.’”

After 18 months of going back and forth with Isagenix, Jessica decided to follow her instincts, order the President’s Pak, and join the company.

After trying the products, she felt invigorated and ended up releasing 12 pounds.*

The Business of Feeling Good

Before learning about Isagenix, she and her brother, Adam, a personal training professional, opened an upscale fitness center.

Now Adam has released 12 pounds* using the products and is sharing them with most of their clients.

“I came home after two months one day and said, ‘Honey, I think I can make millions of dollars with this company,’” laughs this newest Isagenix Millionaire. “He thought I had lost it. He thought I went off the deep end. Joe literally looked at me and said, ‘You think you’re going to make millions of dollars selling protein shakes?’”

Jessica was right!

As a result of her hard work, she recently purchased a luxury SUV, invested in a $100,000 pool for the family, and tripled her daughters’ college savings thanks to her Isagenix business. Her next financial hope is retiring her husband.**

“I will use these products forever—without a doubt,” says Jessica. “I love the idea that my husband and I can be home with our children as they grow up, spending more time as a family. Isagenix has truly been a blessing for us!”
