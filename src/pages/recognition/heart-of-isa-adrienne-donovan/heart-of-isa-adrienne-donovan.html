Congratulations, 2018 Heart of Isagenix Winner, Adrienne Donovan!

Your votes are in, and after weeks of anticipation, Isagenix Owners Jim and Kathy Coover took the stage to award the 2018 Heart of Isagenix winner today at New Year Kick Off. Please join us in congratulating this incredible woman on her remarkable achievement.

This Heart of Isagenix award recipient was nominated for her heart of service and true passion for helping others. She welcomes everyone on her team with open arms, showing them how to be successful in reaching their goals.

Joining us from North Carolina, congratulations to Adrienne Donovan!

“A mom of three beautiful children, a loving wife, sister, daughter, and friend – Adrienne is the most phenomenal team leader I could ever pray for,” said Amanda Smith. “She absolutely deserves this award as she has a heart to serve like none other.”

“When I started this business, I was looking for a solution to raise money for my son who has Down syndrome, and Isagenix has brought us that solution and so much more,” said Adrienne. “I am so humbled and honored. If you are new with Isagenix, just know that you have such a gift here.”*

Thank you for nominating leaders and team members who consistently serve. The hundreds of nominations we received are proof that we have an unmatched culture that is truly impacting lives. Stay tuned to IsaFYI.com/Recognition for more voters’ choice recognition opportunities!
